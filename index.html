Why this exists.

Every other app wants you to do more. More tabs. More projects. More tags. More notifications.

This one is built around a quiet observation: most days, there is one thing that actually matters. The rest is comfort and avoidance.

So we removed the rest. No accounts. No teams. No sync. No streaks to maintain anxiously. No tags, no priorities, no projects, no inbox.

Just the thing. Until it's done.

Then the next thing.

If this feels too small, that's the point. The tool isn't doing the work — you are. We're just trying to stay out of the way.

— made with restraint
